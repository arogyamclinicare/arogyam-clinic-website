## 📱 **MOBILE CONSIDERATIONS**

### **✅ Advantages:**
- Faster initial page load
- Better organization for large content
- Easier to add new sections

### **❌ Disadvantages:**
- Page transition delays
- More complex navigation
- SEO spread across multiple pages
- Higher bounce rates
